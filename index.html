Privacy Policy

AdWatcher – Effective date: May 23, 2026

AdWatcher is developed by Marco Balzarotti. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be collected when you use the app, how that data may be used, and the choices available to users.

Information collected

AdWatcher may store limited information locally on your device in order to provide app functionality. This may include in-app progress, coins, unlocked badges, preferences, and similar gameplay or usage data.

The app may also use third-party services such as Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads to display advertisements. These third-party services may collect certain data automatically, such as device identifiers, advertising ID, IP address, diagnostic information, app interactions, and approximate location derived from IP address, in accordance with their own privacy practices.

How information is used

  • To provide the app's core functionality.
  • To save user progress and preferences.
  • To display and measure advertisements.
  • To improve app stability, performance, and user experience.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, or technical misuse.

Advertising

AdWatcher uses advertising services provided by Google AdMob. Google and its partners may use data collected from your device to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, subject to applicable law, platform settings, and user consent requirements.

You can learn more about how Google uses information here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Children's privacy

AdWatcher is not intended for children under the age of 13, and the developer does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

If you believe that personal information from a child under 13 has been provided through the app, please contact the developer so appropriate action can be taken.

Data sharing

Data may be shared with third-party service providers only to the extent necessary for app functionality, advertising, analytics, diagnostics, legal compliance, or security. This may include Google AdMob and related Google services.

User choices

Depending on your device and region, you may be able to limit ad personalization, reset your advertising ID, or manage privacy settings directly in your Android device settings.

Security

Reasonable efforts are made to reduce risks related to data handling. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Changes to this policy
